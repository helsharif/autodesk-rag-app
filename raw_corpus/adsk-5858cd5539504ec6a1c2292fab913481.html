Benefits and perks

From health and financial benefits to time away and everyday wellness, we give Autodeskers the best, so they can do their best work. 

*Benefits may vary by location.

  • Health and wellness

    We’ve got you covered! From medical, dental, and vision insurance to wellness benefits, we provide options that support a healthy life.

  • Financial

    You invest in us, and we invest in you. We provide robust financial benefits to help you build for the future. 

  • Time away

    Life happens. We get it. That’s why we offer flexible benefits and unlimited time off. Oh, and did we mention sabbatical?

  • Everyday living 

    Life is full of surprises! No matter where you are on life’s journey, we’ve got your back with education, family support, and mental health programs.
