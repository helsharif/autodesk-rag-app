    Frequently asked questions (FAQs)

    What is Prodsmart used for?

    Prodsmart provides manufacturers and fabricators, from small to large-scale, the power to manage every aspect of their production floor. You can use it to get insights into your production flow, identify where waste occurs, predict maintenance, and keep on top of employee performance.

    Who uses Prodsmart?

    Prodsmart is a multi-capable tool used by operations managers, production managers, schedulers, packaging specialists, fulfillment operators, QA teams, and shop floor fabricators.

    What does a manufacturing execution system do?

    A Manufacturing Execution System, or MES, can monitor, track, report, and even control the various processes and systems used to manufacture goods, from raw material to shipping.

    Can MES and ERP work together?

    Yes! Prodsmart brings together MES and ERP capabilities to consolidate the number of tools used, streamline your workflow to require less tools, and better communicate the relevant information back and forth.
