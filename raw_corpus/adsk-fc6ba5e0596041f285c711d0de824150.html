University recruitment

EPIC (Empowering Potential, Inclusivity & Connection) is a pre-internship development program for interns hired through our partnership with NACME  and HBCU 20x20. The purpose is to create a pipeline of underrepresented talent for Autodesk with a focus on mentorship and success coaching for under represented intern talent.
