Autodesk Civil Infrastructure Public Roadmap

Autodesk is continually improving the Infrastructure Portfolio of products including Civil 3D, InfraWorks and ReCap Pro. The roadmap shares a view of the feature development pipeline. We make the public roadmaps available to you—our users—to give you the opportunity to provide feedback, input, and upvote your favorite features. We incorporate your feedback to help consider and prioritize what we build into our product. We would like to continue our engagement with the community. For more on voting on ideas or posting new ideas, read this article in the Autodesk Community Forums.

Our Safe Harbor Statement 

Roadmaps are plans, not promises. We’re as excited as you to see new functionality make it into the products, but the development, releases, and timing of any features or functionality remains at our sole discretion. These roadmap updates should not be used to make purchasing decisions.
