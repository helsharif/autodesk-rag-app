Frequently asked questions

What is Autodesk AI?

Autodesk AI technology is available in Autodesk products and native to Autodesk’s Design and Make Platform. Autodesk AI augments creative exploration and problem-solving, automates tedious and repetitive work, and analyzes project data to offer predictive insights.  

What is Autodesk’s history with AI?

Over the last 10 years, Autodesk has invested in artificial intelligence because we recognize the transformative power of AI for the industries we serve. It’s already helping our customers achieve more with less. Today, Autodesk AI powers workflows and capabilities across Autodesk software and the Autodesk Platform, with more capabilities coming every day.

How do I access Autodesk AI technology?

Autodesk AI is native to Autodesk’s Design and Make Platform and found in many of our desktop and cloud products like AutoCAD, Maya, Fusion, and Forma. We’re developing and acquiring more AI-powered workflows every day.
