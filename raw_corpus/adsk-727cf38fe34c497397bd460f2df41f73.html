SQL Catalog Support for Autodesk Vault

AutoCAD Electrical 2022 now supports the SQL catalog database for the Bill of Materials in Autodesk Vault.

When generating the Bill of Materials in Autodesk Vault for an AutoCAD Electrical project, the data from the AutoCAD Electrical drawings including the details in the catalog database are added to the report.

In addition to Microsoft Access database, the support is extended to the Microsoft SQL format of the catalog.
