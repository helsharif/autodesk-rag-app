Showcase

Showcase is no longer available for purchase

As of March 21, 2017, Autodesk is ending the sale of Showcase® 3D visualization and 3D presentation software to new customers. Autodesk provides several design visualization solutions for different industries, from in-product visualization to cloud visualization services.

Learn more about the changes and alternate products

 

Consider these alternate products:

Rendering in A360 : is a simple cloud rendering service available from the Rendering tab in AutoCAD, Revit, Navisworks, and 3ds Max (may require cloud credits).

3ds Max® : provides design visualization for specialists creating advanced renderings with animations, character, crowd sequences, and visual effects.

VRED™ Design : is an interactive design exploration and presentation tool for digital modelers and visualization teams in the automotive industry.

Revit® Live : is an interactive visualization service offers Revit users the ability to transform their designs into interactive 3D models.

Inventor® : provides in-product visualization workflows for product reviews, rendering, and animation directly within the software.
