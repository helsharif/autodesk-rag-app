# Careers | Autodesk

### Why Autodesk?

Do you like to think outside the box? Seize opportunities? Imagine and achieve new possibilities? We’ve got a hunch you’ll love it at Autodesk.

Our software spans countless industries and helps shape a better, more sustainable world. But we’re always restless to do more. We need problem solvers. Visionaries. Creators. Innovators. People like you!

[Meet our leaders](https://www.autodesk.com/company/newsroom/corporate-info/executive-bios)

### Build your career here

We need people of all experiences and skills to shape a thriving future for everyone. Ready to push boundaries and challenge the status quo? You can do that, and more, at Autodesk.

### Tech jobs for technologists

We invent for the innovators. Engineer for the builders. Reimagine for the creators. Our software solutions enable the people who design and make the world around us. We provide our technologists with endless opportunities to learn, grow, and do the best work of their lives. Because when you harness and apply your talent here, you can help shape the world!

### Sales jobs for the customer-obsessed

How would you like to build a business that helps build the world? Our sales and customer success teams do more than sell. We partner with customers to provide solutions that create more sustainable buildings, smarter products, and mesmerizing motion pictures. Here, you’ll find supportive teams, inspiring customers, and endless possibilities.

[Learn more about working in sales](https://www.autodesk.com/careers/sales)

### Business jobs for visionaries

From HR and finance to legal, in a business role at Autodesk, you help set the foundation. Drive our priorities. Champion the vision. You play an essential role in creating and executing our strategy. And every Autodesker is empowered to grow here, reinvent themselves, and make their mark.

## Benefits and perks

From health and financial benefits to time away and everyday wellness, we give Autodeskers the best, so they can do their best work.

* Benefits may vary by location.

- Health and wellness We’ve got you covered! From medical, dental, and vision insurance to wellness benefits, we provide options that support a healthy life.

- Financial You invest in us, and we invest in you. We provide robust financial benefits to help you build for the future.

- Time away Life happens. We get it. That’s why we offer flexible benefits and unlimited time off. Oh, and did we mention sabbatical?

- Everyday living Life is full of surprises! No matter where you are on life’s journey, we’ve got your back with education, family support, and mental health programs.

## Work where you’re most productive

We believe flexibility in where and how work gets done helps us thrive and realize our potential. Our hybrid-first approach allows us to meet the needs of our business while driving productivity, connection, and belonging. Autodeskers have the flexibility to work where they’re most productive, whether that’s office-based, hybrid, or home-based.
