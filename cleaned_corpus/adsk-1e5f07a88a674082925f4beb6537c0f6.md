# Software Bundle | OEM Software Partner Program | Autodesk

Reach global markets with your complete, unique, industry-specific applications. Under the terms of an ISV software bundle agreement, you supply your customized Autodesk-based application together with our original software.

With an ISV (independent software vendor) Bundle agreement, you are the supplier of a bundled solution—subject to Autodesk approval of your business plan.

OEM Bundle partners receive discounts on Autodesk software. This special pricing applies to all our purchasing models, so you receive discounts regardless of whether you buy Autodesk® Subscription, or full commercial versions.

Note that with a Bundle agreement, your application and our software must be sold together. A software bundle can also be a combination of a standard Autodesk solution with your machine or support a franchising system.
