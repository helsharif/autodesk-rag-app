# TruComposites is no longer available

As of May 7, 2021, Autodesk will no longer offer new subscriptions for Autodesk TruComposites. One year subscription renewal is available until January 25, 2022.

## Continued support for TruComposites

As a subscriber, you can continue to use TruComposites, receive support, and access previous versions of your software until your service contract ends. Talk with your Autodesk partner or sales representative for more information.

[TruComposites support](https://knowledge.autodesk.com/support/trucomposites-products)
