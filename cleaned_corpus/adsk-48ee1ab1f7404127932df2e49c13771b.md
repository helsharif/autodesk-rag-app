# Autodesk Student Downloads and Resources

### Embrace the future. Lead the change.

With advanced technologies and workflows accelerating change in industries and careers, we at Autodesk are excited to partner with you on your professional journey. We invest in students because we believe your ideas and innovation have the power to make this world a better place for everyone. Lead the change and change the world.

### Access the Autodesk Education plan

Confirm your eligibility to get access to free Autodesk products for educational use. Follow the steps in the [Student's guide to the education plan](https://www.autodesk.com/support/account/education/onboarding/students-guide) .

## Learning resources

### Learn Autodesk products

Find tutorials, product guides, certifications, and more. Resources for wherever you're at in your learning journey.

### Connect with the community

Check out the student hub to learn about the latest industry trends, connect with other members, and showcase your work.

### Get help and resources

Find answers to frequently asked questions about the Autodesk Education plan and get product documentation and support.

## Ready to make anything? Start here

### Tinkercad

Tinkercad is a free web app for 3D design, electronics, and coding. Build STEM confidence with project-based learning.

### Instructables

Join the community and explore easy-to-learn instructional projects for makers of all skill levels—or create a project of your own.

### Fusion 360

Fusion 360 is a cloud-based 3D modeling, CAD, CAM, CAE, and PCB software platform for professional design and manufacturing.

## Showcase your skills with certifications

### Autodesk Certified User (ACU)

For secondary students and non-English speakers in all industries, this certification validates foundational Autodesk software skills.

### Design and manufacturing

Prove your skills in CAD, CAM, design-to-manufacture workflows, and generative design with these Associate, Professional, and Expert certifications.

### Architecture, engineering, and construction

Advance your career in architecture, engineering, and construction by highlighting your knowledge of CAD and BIM tools.
