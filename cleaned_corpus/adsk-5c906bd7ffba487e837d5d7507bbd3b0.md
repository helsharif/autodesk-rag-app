# Combining 2D and 3D Workflows for Faster, More Accurate Takeoffs

One of the best ways to maximize the benefits of BIM technology (including time saved, more accurate data, and reduced rework) is by moving from 2D to 3D workflows. In this guide, we explore strategies estimators can use to promote the adoption of 3D workflows across their organization.

You’ll learn:

- How to choose the right software that drives better collaboration
- The best strategies to tackle internal opposition to new technology
- Proven tips for more successful technology adoption from project partners

Plus, get exclusive insights from our survey to over 1,000 preconstruction professionals!
