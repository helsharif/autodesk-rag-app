# Autodesk Certificate of Insurance | Autodesk

## Evidence of insurance

In order to help our customers and business partners reduce the overall impact on the environment, we have discontinued issuing individual certificates of insurance in favor of the new industry standard - an electronic certificate of insurance (e-COI). This page will provide 24-hour access to Autodesk's evidence of insurance. Autodesk is often requested by outside parties to provide evidence of corporate insurance coverage. Examples of situations where Autodesk may be required to provide evidence of insurance are as follows:

- Using a non-Autodesk location to host an event

- Leasing or renting equipment, motor vehicle(s), or real estate

- Customer RFPs and contracts
