# Technology And Developer Partners | Autodesk

## Autodesk Platform Services (formerly Forge)

Autodesk Platform Services (formerly Forge) is a scalable developer platform for creating connected, data-centric AEC and manufacturing solutions using Autodesk cloud-based software components. Improve existing software or build new or custom solutions and experiences using standard web technologies.

## Autodesk Developer Network (ADN)

Build your relationship with us by developing desktop solutions that extend Autodesk products and technologies. Get access to resources and services you can use to develop, test, support, and market your solutions.

## Design and Manufacturing Partnerships

Create and deliver hardware and software solutions, cloud offerings, services, and more to help design and manufacturing companies achieve better business outcomes. Connect with millions of users across multiple industries and functions, and explore exciting new joint go-to-market possibilities.

## Autodesk App Store

Publish your apps and industry solutions in the Autodesk App Store, the online resource where millions of Autodesk customers worldwide browse, download, and purchase apps they need.

*Program may not be available in all regions.

## Independent Software Vendors (ISVs)

Access the sales, marketing, and technical resources you need to develop, integrate, and promote your software, hardware, and machinery solutions that integrate with Autodesk.*
