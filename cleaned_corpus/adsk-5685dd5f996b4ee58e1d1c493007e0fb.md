# Autodesk Build Increases Efficiency and Collaboration for The BOLDT Company

Connected workflows with Autodesk Build and BIM Collaborate within Autodesk Construction Cloud™ is helping The BOLDT Company reduce waste, drive efficiencies, and increase collaboration on the AMG Park Ridge project. With project information saved in real-time on one platform, teams can design and build with confidence that they’re working off the latest project information.
