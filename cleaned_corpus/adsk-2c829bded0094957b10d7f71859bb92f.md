# TruFiber | Autodesk

As of October 12, 2018, Autodesk has combined Autodesk® TruFiber with Autodesk® TruComp to form Autodesk® TruComposites software.

All of the functionality of TruFiber and TruComp can now be accessed in TruComposites.

Existing customers can renew their current subscriptions of TruFiber or [migrate to TruComposites](/products/trucomposites/overview) to begin enjoying the benefits of the collective product.

All TruFiber functionality exists in TruComposites with the added benefit of the TruComp functionality. The price of TruComposites will remain the same as the current yearly subscription price of TruFiber.
