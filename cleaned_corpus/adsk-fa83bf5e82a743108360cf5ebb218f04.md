# OEM Software Partner Program | Autodesk

The Autodesk OEM software Partner Program is our portfolio of Build, Bundle, and Integrate partnership agreements for third-party solution developers, or ISVs (independent software vendors).

The Autodesk OEM Software Partner Program is our portfolio of commercial partnership agreements for third-party solution developers.

Currently used by many partners worldwide, the program is exclusively for those who develop—or want to develop—customized applications based on Autodesk software.

As an OEM software partner, you gain more than easy access to the world’s leading design tools. You also get programming information, training, and marketing support. Most importantly, you benefit from the reputation, market reach, and expertise of a software partner with an installed base of approximately 7 million users.

The OEM Software Partner Program consists of two distinct commercial agreement models- [Build](https://www.autodesk.com/partner-program/customized-applications) and [Bundle](https://www.autodesk.com/partner-program/bundle). [Build](https://www.autodesk.com/partner-program/customized-applications) is the classical OEM program and includes [AutoCAD OEM](https://www.techsoft3d.com/products/autocad-oem/), [Inventor OEM](https://www.techsoft3d.com/products/inventor-oem/) and the [RealDWG SDK](https://www.techsoft3d.com/products/realdwg/). Licensing is handled globally by our partner [Tech Soft 3D](https://www.techsoft3d.com/solutions/oem-platforms). [Bundle](https://www.autodesk.com/partner-program/bundle) is the full product bundled with your software solution or machine. Learn more about ISV Bundles [here](https://www.autodesk.com/partner-program/bundle).

The OEM Partner Program is a unique opportunity to develop customized applications based on design tools. In many cases, these tools are products used by millions around the world.

A partnership with Autodesk can give you a solid business foundation. We have an installed base of approximately 7 million users in more than 160 countries, and many of our products—AutoCAD, AutoCAD Map 3D, Revit, 3ds Max, and Maya—are industry-leading.

Extend your distribution channels into untapped markets and countries. The OEM Software Partner Program enables you to buy versions of our software in many different languages.

There is no mass market. Instead, there are multiple niche markets, each with very specific needs. An Autodesk OEM partnership helps you develop and market niche solutions and enables you to offer combined technology at competitive prices

Access industry-leading Internet, workflow, and 3D tools and technologies. More and more customers want to visualize and analyze their projects before they are real. We can help you meet these demands.

Save time and money going to market with customized applications. Once you are there, we support you with training and marketing resources.
