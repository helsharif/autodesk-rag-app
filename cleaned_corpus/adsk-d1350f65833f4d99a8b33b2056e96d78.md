# Students | Autodesk

## Students and new grads

Autodesk internships aren’t about organizing files and going on coffee runs. Here, you get to work on meaningful projects, be mentored by industry leaders, work with peers from colleges and universities around the globe, and participate in tech talks, project showcases, and other activities that will really enhance your personal and professional development. It’s an experience you’ll never forget!

## Internship/co-op durations

Internships vary by global region, but they typically last at least 12 weeks, with recruitment taking place during the fall and spring.

United States – 3 months (summer)

Canada – 16 weeks (summer, fall, winter)

Europe – 3-6 months (summer)

Asia-Pacific – 3-6 months (summer, fall, winter)

Hiring needs can change often, so be sure to keep coming back to [check out our open positions](https://autodesk.wd1.myworkdayjobs.com/uni)!

### How do interviews work at Autodesk?

While things may differ slightly by role, here’s an idea of what to expect:

Step 1: Preparation Learn about Autodesk on our website and social media channels. Think about how your skills and experience align with a career here.

Step 2: Interviews You will have 2-3 virtual interviews with the hiring manager and team members. Here’s where you can show why you’re a good fit for the role and ask questions.

Step 3: Assessment You may be asked to take a technical assessment – this could be a simple coding question, or a 30–60-minute coding exercise.

Step 4: Decision Once all interviews are complete, you will hear back from your recruiter with an outcome within two weeks.

*If you have questions throughout the process, please connect with your recruiter.*
