# The Ultimate Guide to Leveraging Data in your Construction Lifecycle

The reliance on and sheer volume of project data have been increasing over the last few years within the construction industry. Retaining all project data from start to finish is a key consideration of today’s project owners and general contractors.

In this ebook you will learn:

- How to keep all project data in one system or software platform
- Cut down on the amount of data that is lost throughout the project
- Make data available in one place for every stakeholder
- Streamline the commissioning and handover processes at completion
