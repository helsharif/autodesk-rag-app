# TruLaser | Autodesk

As of October 12, 2018, Autodesk has combined Autodesk® TruLaser with Autodesk® TruPlan to form Autodesk® TruComposites software.

All of the functionality of TruLaser and TruPlan can now be accessed in TruComposites.

Existing customers can renew their current subscriptions of TruLaser or [migrate to TruComposites](/products/trucomposites/overview) to begin enjoying the benefits of the collective product.

All TruLaser functionality exists in TruComposites with the added benefit of the TruPlan functionality.

And if you purchase TruComposites in addition to the Product Design & Manufacturing Collection, you get all the tools that work right inside Inventor—including advanced simulation, 5-axis CAM, and nesting—along with AutoCAD and Fusion 360 for less than the current yearly subscription price of TruLaser.
