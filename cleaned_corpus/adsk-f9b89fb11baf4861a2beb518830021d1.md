# Autodesk Administrators | Tasks and Resources | Upgrade Products and Plans

You can upgrade an LT product to full product. If your product is part of an industry collection, you can upgrade to a collection.

If your subscription is on a Standard plan, you can upgrade to a Premium plan.

## Upgrade a product

Upgrade to an industry collection or from an LT product to a full product. The way you upgrade depends on how you got your subscription.

**Online**: If you purchased your product online, turn off[automatic renewal](/support/account/manage/billing/renew). When the subscription expires purchase the full product or collection[online](/products). For immediate access to the full product or collection,[download a free 30-day trial](/free-trials)to use before your product expires.**Autodesk sales**or**Reseller**: If you got your product from an[Autodesk sales representative](/benefits/sales-support)or[authorized partner](/partners/locate-a-reseller), contact them for assistance.

**Set up your industry collection**

Usually, your users don’t need to reinstall their software. And you don't need to update serial numbers unless you upgraded from a 2019 or earlier stand-alone product. Updating the serial number may result in an [Invalid serial number](https://knowledge.autodesk.com/search-result/caas/sfdcarticles/sfdcarticles/Error-Serial-number-invalid.html) error. In the rare case that you do need to update your serial number, see [Update a serial number](/support/download-install/individuals/manage/update-a-serial-number).

**Note**: If you subscribe to a different product through Autodesk sales or a reseller, switch from a Design & Creation Suite, and your current subscription lapsed, you do need to reinstall your software.

## Assign upgraded products

Your users can continue using the old software for 30 days from the order date as you complete the switch. In your account, look for instructions to guide you through the trade-in process.

[Assign product access](/support/account/admin/users/assign-access)to the new subscription. You can use filters to sort users by contract.- (optional) Unassign users from the original subscription.

For new products, your users receive an email that they can sign in to their account to download products.

## Upgrade to a Premium plan

Admins can upgrade to a Premium plan for more administrative features and support options. See [Autodesk plans](/plans) to compare Standard and Premium and the [plans FAQ](https://www.autodesk.com/plans#faq) for questions about pricing and eligible products.

To upgrade your plan,

[contact us](/campaigns/premium-plan#contact-form)or your reseller.If you've already purchased a Premium plan, see

[How to set up your Premium plan](/support/account/admin/plans/premium-plan).
