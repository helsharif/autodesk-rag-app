# Autodesk Education Community Resources

## Join our education community

### Students

Get free student access to Autodesk products, grow your design and engineering skills, and get certified. Lead the change and change the world.

Learn about our resources, deployment offerings, and onboarding for Autodesk technology and understand our privacy and security landscape.

## Resources for students & educators

### [Comprehensive learning content](https://www.autodesk.com/education/educators-learning-resources)

Learn the skills for emerging roles and future careers with Autodesk’s industry-validated learning content. Discover the content that's perfect for your learning journey or curriculum.

### [Education community forum](https://forums.autodesk.com/t5/student-group-hub/gh-p/student-group-hub)

Join us to learn about the latest industry trends, connect with other members, showcase your work, and more! Learn from & give back to the community.

Find support for account configuration, installation, and more by chatting with a virtual agent or describing your question for individual web case support.
