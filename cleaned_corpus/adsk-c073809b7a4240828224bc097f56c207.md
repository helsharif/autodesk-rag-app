# Account Basics | Using Autodesk Account | Autodesk Support

[https://www.autodesk.com/plans](/plans).

## Contact support

- Select a path on the
[Contact Support page](https://knowledge.autodesk.com/contact-support)to be guided to the right Autodesk support agent. - Sign in to
[Autodesk Account](https://manage.autodesk.com/home/)and click the ? icon to access help and support. - Students and educators can see support options on the
[Autodesk Education Support page](/education/support).

**Note:** Enterprise Priority customers can click the Support Cases tile at the top of their Account home page.

## View your support cases

- Sign in to
[Autodesk Account](https://manage.autodesk.com/home). - Click View My Support Cases on the home page.
