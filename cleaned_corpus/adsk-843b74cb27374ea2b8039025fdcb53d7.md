# Getting started with Info360 Asset: a new video playlist - One Water Blog

[Info360 Asset](https://www.autodesk.com/products/info360-asset/overview) is our cloud-based application that helps asset practitioners at utilities and municipalities better manage costly inspection data, use historical and current condition data to monitor and assess condition and performance of their assets, and build models of likelihood of failure (LoF) and consequence of failure (CoF) for renewal prioritization planning.

We’re always adding new functionality and features to Info360 Asset, which means that from time to time, we need to update the way we keep our customers informed about all the options available in the software. We want new users of Info360 Asset to have an easy onboarding process, which is why we’ve created a new set of getting started videos.

## Subscribe to our YouTube channel for new videos

You can watch the videos [on our support portal](https://help.autodesk.com/view/INF360A/ENU/?guid=GUID-EBCD3387-1E11-4596-8129-8BD2DC9FA3FB) or watch them right on this page [via this YouTube playlist](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1ti10XmGea0&list=PLxZQHvxMelqu_-zaiC2izM4U6zOccFHfj). If you stop by YouTube, consider subscribing to our YouTube channel and turn notifications on to be notified whenever we publish a new video.
