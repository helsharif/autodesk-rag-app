# Autodesk Developer Network membership

## Partner with us to fuel your software innovation

Build your relationship with Autodesk by joining the Autodesk Developer Network (ADN), a developer program for Autodesk Platforms—desktop software and cloud solutions. Whether you're new to software development or a professional software developer new to Autodesk, a software business start-up, or well-established company with a team of full-time professional software developers, ADN can help you develop specialized solutions by extending Autodesk technology or integrating your solutions with it.

### ADN membership benefits

The ADN membership is for full-time professional software developers working with Autodesk platform technologies who need access to Autodesk software and one-on-one direct support for software development based on virtually all Autodesk technologies.

- Access to virtually all Autodesk software for development, support, and field demonstration purposes
- Access to pre-release beta versions of future Autodesk software releases
- One-on-one technical support from an Autodesk software engineer
- Access to confidential information
- Access to Autodesk technology conferences around the world (regular attendee event registration fees will be applicable)
- Access to Autodesk customers and sales teams through exhibition at Autodesk University (regular exhibitor and attendee event registration fees will be applicable)

### ADN membership pricing

- 1 user*: 1,750
-
2 to 5 users*: 3,000

-
6 or more users*: 5,500

**US$** in Americas, CIS and Asia | **CAD$** in Canada | **Euros** in Europe/Middle East/Africa | **AUS$** in Australia and New Zealand

(pricing effective January 1, 2024)

*User(s) is the number of employees that will be using ADN-provided Autodesk desktop software, web services, and support services. This includes your software engineers, support personnel, and for in-the-field customer demonstrations. There are 3 user levels to choose from: 1 user, 2 to 5 users, and 6 or more users. ADN membership runs per calendar year—January 1 to December 31—and is not prorated.

ADN membership pricing may vary in some countries, including India, Japan, the People’s Republic of China, and a number of countries in Eastern Europe, the Middle East, and Africa.

## Resources

### Qualify for free or reduced ADN membership

-
Companies joining ADN between October 1 and December 31 receive a membership for the following year at no extra cost.

-
Authors of learning materials including books, video, and online learning aids receive special pricing for an ADN membership. Contact your local Autodesk Developer Network representative for details.

-
University developers qualify to receive ADN membership at no charge to support their engineering and design-related research, and software development efforts.

-
Start-up developers qualify to receive ADN membership at no charge for up to 3 years to support their startup projects and software development efforts. You can apply to join ADN now under the start-up developer category directly by submitting an online

[ADN membership application.](https://adndata.autodesk.io/sysinit.asp?SessionType=Applicant)

If you have questions about the Autodesk Developer Network membership, [contact your local Autodesk Developer Network representative](https://aps.autodesk.com/autodesk-developer-programs/adn/contact-adn).

### Access Autodesk software at no cost

Trial versions are available for most Autodesk products. Download your free 30-day trial version today. You can get Autodesk software for development use on an on-going basis by joining ADN.
