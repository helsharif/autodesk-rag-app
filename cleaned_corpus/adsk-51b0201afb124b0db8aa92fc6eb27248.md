Estimators are a project’s first safeguard against risks like rework and budget overruns. Creating accurate takeoffs quickly, collaborating effectively with team members and project stakeholders, and implementing construction technology provide a vital competitive edge for any estimator.

In this guide, we dive into the habits of highly effective estimators, including:

-
Powering collaboration through technology

-
Staying on top of version control

-
Streamlining the takeoff process

-
Utilizing both 2D and 3D models to create accurate takeoffs.
