## About the Technology Centers

Autodesk Technology Centers welcome video (2:06 min.)

## Building a shared vision of the future

The organizations and teams who engage with the Autodesk Technology Centers conduct speculative and industry-applied work pertaining to design and make. From start-ups and customers, to academics, industry teams and groups within Autodesk, our residents are leaders with a passion for accelerating and researching design and make processes in a myriad of industries.

## The Outsight Network

The Outsight Network is a global community of resident teams from industry, academic, and entrepreneurial sectors who are passionate about accelerating and researching design and make processes. The network offers open access to resources that enable teams to discover and test advanced techniques for design and make.

## State-of-the-art facilities

With a global network that spans North America, Europe, and Asia-Pacific, the Autodesk Technology Centers’ ability to foster connections is made possible through virtual engagements and physical spaces. The Technology Centers Workshops and Studios are physical locations with fabrication equipment and design technology. Each location is equipped with technical experts who can support teams with their use of workshop equipment for projects and initiatives.

## Technology Center locations

- San Francisco The Autodesk Technology Center in San Francisco is located on the Embarcadero at Pier 9 and has a wide range of advanced manufacturing equipment, robotics, and workspaces to explore new ideas around traditional manufacturing.

- Boston Located in Boston’s Seaport Innovation District, the Autodesk Technology Center in Boston provides access to large-format fabrication equipment, a wide range of robotics, ample project space, and training and expertise from Autodesk personnel.

- Toronto Autodesk Technology Centre in Toronto offers access to advance prototyping and validation tools, where industry experts can come together to reimagine how they design the world around us.

- Birmingham, UK The Autodesk Technology Centre in Birmingham, UK, works with customers across industries to help them develop new manufacturing and fabrication techniques.
