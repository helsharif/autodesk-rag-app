## FAQ: Autodesk advanced manufacturing software

[Autodesk Fusion 360](https://www.autodesk.com/products/fusion-360/cnc-machining) provides all the essential tools you need for 2D, 3+2, CAM, and 3D printing, as well as cloud collaboration tools. For more advanced CNC machining, Fusion 360 subscribers can upgrade to include the [Autodesk Fusion 360 Machining Extension](https://www.autodesk.com/products/fusion-360/machining-extension?term=1-YEAR&tab=subscription) . This extension unlocks additional, advanced capabilities to make better use of 3-axis and multi-axis CNC machinery, including automated programming, multi-axis milling, and more.

Autodesk provides a range of manufacturing software products. The best option will vary based on your specific needs. In the vast majority of cases, [Autodesk Fusion 360.](https://www.autodesk.com/products/fusion-360/overview) It offers the tools you need for 2D and 3D CAD (design), 2.5D to 5-axis CAM (manufacturing), as well as additive manufacturing ( [3D printing](https://www.autodesk.com/solutions/3d-printing) ), metrology (inspection), simulation (CAE), and much more.

For the most complicated applications, Autodesk provides a suite of specialist products that can be used alongside Fusion 360 to access more advanced tools. These products include:

- [PowerMill](https://www.autodesk.com/products/powermill/overview) CAM for advanced 3- to 5-axis machining

- [FeatureCAM](https://www.autodesk.com/products/featurecam/overview) CAM for turning, turn-mill, and Swiss machining

- [PowerInspect](https://www.autodesk.com/products/powerinspect/overview) to connect with a range of inspection and metrology devices

- [PowerShape](https://www.autodesk.com/products/powershape/overview) modeling for manufacture tools for mold, tool, and die

- [Netfabb](https://www.autodesk.com/products/netfabb/overview) preparing, slicing, and simulating additive manufacturing

- [Prodsmart](https://www.autodesk.com/products/prodsmart/overview) production management, tracking, scheduling, and reporting

- [CAMplete](https://www.autodesk.com/products/camplete/overview) NC-code post-processing and G-code simulation

- [Moldflow](https://www.autodesk.com/products/moldflow/overview) injection and compression molding plastic simulation
