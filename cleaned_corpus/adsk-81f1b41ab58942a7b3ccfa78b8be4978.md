## Fusion 360 features for consumer product development

### Powerful 3D design

Bring designs to life with parametric, surface, direct, freeform, and mesh modeling. Explore new concepts with automated modeling and generative design.

### Easy collaboration

Seamlessly collaborate with cloud-based data ensuring teams access the same data. Web browser access enables access for extended teams and captures design conversations.

### Unified PCB design

Quickly design circuits, visualize your electronic product's 3D model aspects, and test how the PCB fits within the design assembly with ECAD and MCAD integration.

### Integrated CAD/CAM

Manufacture your designs using the integrated CAD/CAM environment which supports 2D, 2.5D, 3-, 4-, and 5-axis CNC milling, turning, turn-mill, and in-cycle probing and inspection.

### Photo-realistic rendering

Communicate designs effectively with photo-realistic renderings and animations. Drag and drop realistic appearances from an extensive materials database.

### Additive & 3D printing

Quickly create physical prototypes to validate prior to manufacturing and build 3D printed parts. Additively manufacture builds using FFF or PBF for metal 3D printing.

### Simulation

Predict product performance and manufacturability with finite element analysis (FEA), injection molding simulation, and electronics cooling analysis.

### Sustainability insights

The Makersite Add-on for Fusion 360 helps innovators design the most sustainable version of their product with insights on CO2 emissions, costs, and material recommendations.
