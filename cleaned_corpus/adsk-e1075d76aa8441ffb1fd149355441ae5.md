# How Engineers and Manufactures can Collaborate to Combat COVID-19

Designers, engineers, medical professionals, and maker communities around the world are developing new solutions for personal protective equipment and life-saving devices.

Have an idea or design you think can help but need to make sure it can be manufactured and approved for its intended use?

Companies around the globe with manufacturing capacity are ready to help with production.

In four weeks, 3-Dimensional manufactured more than 135,000 face-shield headbands for healthcare workers on the front lines.

Washington and Oregon high school FIRST Robotic teams band together to help make PPE for local healthcare workers.

When Tyler Mantel’s startup company was put on hold due to COVID-19, he launched The Ventilator Project to help solve the ventilator shortage.
