# TruPlan | Autodesk

As of October 12, 2018, Autodesk has combined Autodesk® TruPlan with Autodesk® TruLaser to form Autodesk® TruComposites software.

All of the functionality of TruPlan and TruLaser can now be accessed in TruComposites.

Existing customers can renew their current subscriptions of TruPlan or [migrate to TruComposites](/products/trucomposites/overview) to begin enjoying the benefits of the collective product.

All TruPlan functionality exists in TruComposites with the added benefit of the TruLaser functionality.

And if you purchase TruComposites in addition to the Product Design & Manufacturing Collection, you get all the tools that work right inside Inventor—including advanced simulation, 5-axis CAM, and nesting—along with AutoCAD and Fusion 360 for less than the current yearly subscription price of TruPlan.
