# The Big Room | Autodesk Construction Cloud

The Big Room is an online community designed for you to collaborate, ideate and grow with other construction professionals and with Autodesk Construction Cloud™. It’s a place for you to enhance your industry knowledge, converse with Autodesk enthusiasts around the world, and get recognized for the work you do, day-in and day-out.

Whether you are new to Autodesk Construction Cloud or are a seasoned veteran, you all have a voice in The Big Room.

Whether it’s with like-minded people, the Autodesk Construction Cloud team or on stage in front of the industry, The Big Room is a platform for you to share your experience with the world.
