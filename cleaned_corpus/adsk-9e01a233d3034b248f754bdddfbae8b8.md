# Account Basics | Using Autodesk Account | Reset Password

## Reset a password

- Go to
[accounts.autodesk.com](https://accounts.autodesk.com), enter your email address, and click Next.**Note:**If you have an existing account, but can't remember the email address used, do not create a new account. Please[contact us](https://knowledge.autodesk.com/contact-support)for help locating your account sign-in information. If possible, provide a software serial number, subscription contract number, or your admin’s email address to help us locate the correct account. - Click the FORGOT? link above the Password field. You'll receive an email with instructions on resetting your password. If you don't receive the password reset email, check your spam or junk mail folders for an email from noreply@mail.accounts.autodesk.com and add autodesk.com as a trusted email domain. If you still have not received the email, please
[contact support](https://knowledge.autodesk.com/contact-support/account-management/account-creation-sign-profile-management).

## Change your password

- Sign in to your account at
[profile.autodesk.com/security](https://profile.autodesk.com/security). - Click the edit icon in the Password field.
- Enter the current and new password, and then click Save.

**Note:** If you do not see an option to edit your password, your settings are managed by your organization, and you have limited self-service. Contact your organization’s IT or license administrator with any requests.
