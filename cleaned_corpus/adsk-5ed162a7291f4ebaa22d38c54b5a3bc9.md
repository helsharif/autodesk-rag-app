# A Beginner’s Guide to Connecting Construction Data and Documents

Today’s construction sites are plagued by a lack of communication between teams, leading to missed deadlines, costly rework, and a project that becomes famous for all of the wrong reasons. Learn how construction teams are moving beyond paper-based processes, while new technologies are being developed for the construction industry to create a connected job site and eliminate version-control issues.
