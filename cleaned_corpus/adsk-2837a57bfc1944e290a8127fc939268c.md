# Contact Us | Autodesk Trust Center

## I need help with

### Checking a product or service outage

Learn the status of an Autodesk product or service, and view upcoming maintenance for Autodesk cloud services.

[Visit health dashboard](https://health.autodesk.com/)

### Reporting piracy or non-compliance

Report unlicensed use of software, pirate sellers, or distributors of unlicensed software anonymously.

[Contact Autodesk Genuine](https://www.autodesk.com/genuine/report-piracy)

### Reporting a security incident or vulnerability

Share information about an ongoing information security incident, escalate a product vulnerability.

Report security incident

### Requesting an audit or compliance information

Request the full Autodesk SSAE16-SOC2 audit report, or inquire about the compliance status of a product.
